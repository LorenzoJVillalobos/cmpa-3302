# Edward Elric

## Overview

Edward Elric from *Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood* is one of the most complete anime protagonists I have seen because he is talented, flawed, stubborn, and emotionally grounded all at the same time. Edward’s story begins with a mistake: he and his brother Alphonse attempt human transmutation to bring their mother back, and the result costs Edward an arm and a leg while Alphonse loses his entire body. From that point on, Edward’s main goal is to restore what they lost.

## Why His Character Is Strong

Edward is compelling because his intelligence and skill never erase the consequences of his actions. He is gifted, but he is also carrying guilt, responsibility, and the pressure of trying to fix something that cannot be undone easily. That gives his story more weight than a typical action series.

### Traits that define Edward

- intelligent and highly skilled  
- impulsive and stubborn  
- deeply loyal to Alphonse  
- motivated by guilt and responsibility  

> Edward feels believable because his strengths never cancel out his mistakes.

## My Analysis

What I like about Edward is that he is not just fighting villains; he is fighting the consequences of his own choices. That makes his journey feel personal in a way that stands apart from a lot of anime protagonists. He is also one of the best examples of a character who grows emotionally instead of only physically.

Compared with [[monkey-d-luffy]], Edward is much more serious and analytical. Compared with [[denji]], he has a much stronger sense of purpose and discipline. I also think he pairs well with [[tanjiro-kamado]] because both characters are driven by family and sacrifice, even though their personalities are very different. Out of all the characters in this section, Edward may be the most balanced combination of intelligence, emotion, and character development.
